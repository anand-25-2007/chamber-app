About the Advocate

Advocate Amrendra Singh

An alumnus of the prestigious Allahabad University holding B.A., M.A., and LL.B. degrees, Advocate Amrendra Singh has built a distinguished career spanning over two decades. Officially enrolled with the Bar Council in 2002, he operates out of the Judicature of the Allahabad High Court at Lucknow, bringing profound institutional knowledge and rigorous analysis to every brief.

Demonstrating a strong record of public service and legal acumen, he currently serves as Government Counsel and represents the Nagar Palika Parishad (Municipal Council) of Nawabganj, Gonda. He has also been a dedicated, active member of the Oudh Bar Association since 2013.

Legal Philosophy:

Advocate Singh's practice is defined by a strictly thorough, fact-based approach to litigation. He maintains a meticulous standard for accepting matters, requiring:

  • A comprehensive evaluation of the client's total history.
  • Full disclosure of any prior criminal offenses.
  • Detailed analysis of all past disputes between the client and the opposing party.

This uncompromising preliminary analysis ensures that there are no surprises in the courtroom, allowing for the formulation of an airtight and strategic legal defense.
